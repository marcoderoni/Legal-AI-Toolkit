# Workflow: Legal Q&A

## Objective
Answer legal questions from colleagues or business stakeholders,
grounded in the playbook and company positions. Fast, reliable,
consistent with established positions.

## Required Input
- The question, pasted directly in chat
- Context if available (deal size, counterparty, urgency)

## Step 1 — Understand the Question
Identify:
- Is this a contract interpretation question?
- Is this a compliance/regulatory question (GDPR, DORA, AI Act)?
- Is this a "can we accept this clause?" question?
- Is this something else?

## Step 2 — Check Reference Documents
Before answering, consult in this order:
1. references/playbook.docx — check if there is an established position
2. Any relevant template in references/
3. Apply general legal knowledge only if no reference covers the topic

## Step 3 — Answer Format
Produce a structured response:

**Short Answer** (1-2 lines): yes / no / it depends — state the position clearly

**Reasoning** (3-5 lines max): why, grounded in playbook or legal principle

**Recommended Action**: what the colleague should do next

**Escalation flag**: if the question touches a high-risk area per the
decision framework, add:
⚠️ ESCALATE — this requires senior review before proceeding
